Our Services

We offer the service to detect and moderate AI-generated content in environments such as social media platforms, ensuring compliance with rules against automated content generation or identifying potential misuse of GPT-generated text.

Service 1

Educational institutions can use the classifier to identify AI-generated content in assignments, research papers, and essays to maintain academic integrity by distinguishing between student work and AI-assisted writing.

Service 2

Journalists, news outlets, and fact-checking organizations can deploy the classifier to ensure that content submitted or published is human-authored, preventing the spread of disinformation or automated, non-verified content in the media industry.

Service 3

Businesses using AI chatbots for customer support can leverage the classifier to distinguish between chatbot responses and human-agent responses. This helps monitor the quality and appropriateness of automated replies and ensures seamless transitions between AI and human support when needed.
